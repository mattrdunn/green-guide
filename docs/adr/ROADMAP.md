# Roadmap

Directional, not committed. Ordered roughly by priority — not a promise of sequencing.

## Accounts & Personalization

- User accounts (email/password, profile picture, notification preferences)
- Favorites / saved plants
- User-created lists, public or private (e.g. "Easiest Houseplants for Beginners")
- Order/reorder history if a product surface ships (see Monetization below)

## Layout Planners

- **Outdoor:** map land dimensions, home outline, retaining walls, and plantable areas; account for location (zone, daylight hours) and per-section sunlight exposure; recommend suitable plants and care products
- **Indoor:** mock up rooms with window placement, cardinal orientation, and light obstructions; recommend houseplant placement based on light needs


## Generative AI Assistant ("Anys")

- Conversational Q&A grounded in the species database
- Image upload for plant identification and visual symptom/ailment detection
- Recommends in-app reference pages and, where relevant, products — framed as guidance, not upsell


## Monetization (exploratory)

- Contextually recommended products (e.g. inline links within care guidance — fertilizer type linking to a specific product) rather than intrusive ad placements
- Subscription tier: member-only deals, free shipping, etc.

## Non-functional targets

- No-login browsing preserved as a hard requirement even as accounts ship
- Fast page loads / query response times maintained as the catalog and feature surface grow
- GDPR-compliant data handling; cookie policy and consent settings for analytics/marketing once those are added
- TLS everywhere; salted password storage once accounts ship
